Who Are We? Meet The Team

Who We Are

Capital Aid is a student-led, not-for-profit organization based in Ottawa, Canada.

We help international and immigrant students adapt to the Canadian education system through free, high-quality tutoring. Founded by high school students, our mission is to bridge educational gaps and empower academic success.

By Students, For Students

Founded and run by passionate peers who understand the challenges

Not-For-Profit Mission

Committed to keeping education accessible

Academic Integration

Specialized support for adapting to Canadian curriculum

Our Mission

Education

Empowering international and immigrant students by providing accessible, high-quality tutoring to help them navigate the Canadian education system with confidence.

Mentorship

Connecting students with experienced peers who provide academic guidance, and insights into success in Canadian schools.

Tutoring

Offering personalized tutoring to strengthen core subject skills, improve study habits, and prepare students for academic excellence.
